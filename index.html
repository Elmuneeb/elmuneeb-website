Welcome

Welcome to EL‑MUNEEB AGRIVENTURE LTD — an innovative agribusiness based in Azare, Nigeria. We empower smallholder farmers through sustainable agriculture, renewable solutions, and inclusive agritech.

Azolla Innovation

We produce Azolla as an eco‑friendly livestock feed to reduce costs and improve nutrition for farmers across Nigeria. Our model supports sustainability and circular agriculture.

Our Impact

We are reducing feed costs by over 35 % for local farmers, reaching more than 100 rural stakeholders and building pathways to prosperity.
